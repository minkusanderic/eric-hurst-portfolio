About Me

I believe that software can bring people together. I enjoy enabling others to create, share, and connect through the amazing world of computing. I believe that code itself is an art, and I strive each day to become a master of my craft.

I am a recent grad from the University of Utah. I have been interested in computer programming and software engineering since my high school days. I taught myself by checking books out of the library and teaching myself BASIC. From there I graduated to more mature languages like Java and C/C++. After Completing the AP Computer Science tests in high school, I knew that I wanted to work in software.

I have worked a variety of different roles and in a variety of different contexts over the past few years, from web development to embedded systems. I have a wide array of experience and knowledge of different domains. My strong areas include working with Python, and back-end web development. This site itself is programmed using Django and python.
